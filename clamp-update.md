The Clamps mechanic is to be updated.

There are different kinds of clamps and clamp setups.

The single clamp setup places a single clamp in the middle of the containers face which faces away from the train. These have high hitpoints.

The double clamp setup has three possible positions, two of which randomly get a single clamp each. The positions are in the middle of the three faces (top, bottom, away from train facing). These have less hitpoints, only requiring a single hit with the basic starting player gun. 

The triple clamp setup places three clamps evenly spaced on the top of the container. They are long clamps covering the whole width of the top of the container.

The four clamp setup places one clamp on each corner of the containers away from train facing side.

The chances for each clamp type are determined in the settings.

The clamp setup is determined per carriage, and applied to both containers that are attached to that carriage.
